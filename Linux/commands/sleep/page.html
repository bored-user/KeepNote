The "sleep" command adds a delay between the execution of commands.
Its unit can be (default) seconds or a specificed one (seconds, minutes, hours or days).
E.g.:
  • sleep 1 (sleep for 1 second) OR sleep 1s
  • sleep 1m (sleep for 1 minute)
  • sleep 1h (sleep for 1 hour)
  • sleep 1d (sleep for 1 day)
